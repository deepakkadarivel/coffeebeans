about us

Company was born to help businesses communicate with their customers. Since incorporation in 2017, we enabled companies like Thoughtworks, Go-Jek, Zwibe and Appcleus to deliver conversational experience to their clients.

We set on a mission to build first class products that can help companies deliver a more efficient customer service.

Our team follow agile practice giving you the confidence to launch exceptional products. We work on Mobile & Web Development, UI/UX design, Machine Learning, BigData, Artificial Intelligence, Dev Ops, Operations, Quality analysis & Testing, Product Management, Business Analysis and Project Consulting

We create digital experience for brands focusing on unique services provided to their customers. Creativity we make it personal. Let our team become your partner in revolutionizing your business brand identity.

how we work

Small teams working on big challenges in transparent collaboration with our clients. We keep ourselves updated so that we can give you the latest and best technology.

01. Research and Discovery.

We investigate your project and make sure we are on the same page as you. Everything clear? Great! we move forward with the design. A User Interface Specialist (UIX) will work on basic functionality as well as analyze competitive sites to help you gain an edge in your current marketplace.

02. Design and Development.

Our talented team of designers will gather high resolution images, unique fonts, icons and animate that are custom tailored to your specific industry. After the design files are approved, it is time to let our developers tie all the pieces together. All code is original and unique. We hire only the most talented individuals who are leaders and innovators in our industry.

03. Testing.

We test across all software architectures for quality, scale and performance in order to ensure that your application can support massive surge of traffic without compromising the user experience.
